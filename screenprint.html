What I Did

In this project, I learned how screenprinting works by going through each step of the process. I helped create a design and turned it into a stencil that could be used for printing. I also learned how ink is carefully pushed through the screen to transfer the design onto a surface.

I also helped prepare materials and made sure everything was lined up correctly before printing. I worked with classmates to complete the process and make sure the final print came out clearly. It required focus because small mistakes could change the final design.

What I Learned

I learned that screenprinting requires patience and attention to detail. Every step matters, from preparing the stencil to applying the ink. If one step is rushed, the final product will not come out correctly.

I also learned how teamwork is important because everyone has a role in making the project successful. This project helped me understand how art and real production processes are connected in real life.

Skills I Improved

I improved my creativity by designing something that could be turned into a print. I also improved my patience because the process takes time and cannot be rushed.

I also improved my ability to follow step-by-step instructions and stay organized. I learned how to use tools more carefully and pay attention to small details.
