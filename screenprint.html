Our Shared Canvas: Screenprinting Project

What I Did

During the screenprinting project, I learned how designs can be transferred onto different materials using ink and screens. I helped prepare the design and watched how each layer was printed. This project taught me how screenprinting is used to create products and artwork.

I learned the importance of patience and attention to detail. Small mistakes can affect the final design, so every step must be completed carefully.

21st Century Skills

The skill I improved the most was collaboration. I worked with classmates to complete different parts of the project and solve problems together.

I also improved my creativity by helping create and evaluate designs before printing them.
